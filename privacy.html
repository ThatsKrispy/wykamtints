Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Wykam Tints ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you use our website or request a quote, and how we handle it.

What we collect

When you submit our booking or quote form, we collect the information you give us directly:

  • Your name, phone number and email address
  • The service and package you're interested in
  • Vehicle or project details you choose to share

We may also collect basic, non-identifying analytics such as pages visited and general device type to understand how the site is used.

How we use it

  • To respond to your quote request and schedule your appointment
  • To send you your price, confirmation and care instructions
  • To improve our website and services

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with service providers that help us operate (for example, scheduling or email tools), and only as needed to serve you.

Text and email

By submitting the form, you agree we may contact you by phone, text or email about your request. You can opt out of marketing messages at any time by replying STOP to a text or unsubscribing from an email. Appointment-related messages may still be sent.

Cookies

Our site may use simple cookies to remember preferences and measure traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work as well if you do.

Data retention

We keep quote and customer information for as long as needed to provide our services, honor warranties and meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymize it.

Your choices

You may ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you. Contact us using the details below and we'll respond promptly.
